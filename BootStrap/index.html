Who We Are


Youth Is The Future offers a safe creative space for at risk and troubled youth to creativly explore.

Professional teaching artists in well-equipped studios, provide hands-on instruction in a range of subject areas within the visual, performing and new media arts. We support community growth by engaging families and members of the community through programs, performances, and events hosted in our creative space dedicated to bringing high-quality arts education to students of all ages and backgrounds.
